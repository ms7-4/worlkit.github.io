French Croissants

Ingredients:

  • 250g all-purpose flour
  • 125ml cold milk
  • 150g unsalted butter
  • 50g sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 7g dry yeast

Steps:

  1. Activate yeast with warm milk and a pinch of sugar.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, salt, and add the yeast mixture.
  3. Knead dough, then chill for 30 mins.
  4. Roll out dough, layer with butter, fold, and repeat to create layers.
  5. Cut into triangles, roll, and bake at 200Â°C for 15-20 mins until golden.

Enjoy your homemade French croissants with jam or chocolate!
